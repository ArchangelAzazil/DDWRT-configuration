How to install Hotspot Shield on your DD-WRT Router




  1. Please log in to your account on Hotspot Shield website Hotspot Shield


  2. Go to your home page, click on Hotspot Shield VPN on the left side, below your Home Icon
  3. Scroll down until you see the 'Download now' button
  4. Click on 'Router'


  5. Select your preferred virtual location and click on 'Download file'. Take note of the Username and Password and don't close this page.
  6. Go back to the router setup page.


  7. We are using the Netgear router R7000 with DD-WRT Firmware Version 3.0-r53714 (10/20/23)
  8. Connect your router using a LAN cable to your ISP Modem


  9. Please make sure that IPV6 is disabled and click 'Save'


  10. Click on the Services tab and choose VPN


  11. Enable the OpenVPN Client. You will find that a lot of the fields are populated with default values. We can ignore them for now. They will be changed later. Scroll Down until you reach the 'Import Configuration' Option


  12. Click on 'Choose File' and select the configuration you downloaded from the Hotspot Shield website and clik on 'Open'


  13. As soon as you click 'Open', you will find that most of the fields are now populated with the required configuration for our router, including the certificates, public and private key. You do not need to change any of these. But you will need to enter your Username and Password.


  14. Go back to the Hotspot Sheild website where you downloaded the configuration file. Copy the Username and Password and enter them in the Username and Password fields for the router.


  15. Click on 'Save' then click on 'Apply Settings'. Please give this a few seconds to update.


  16. Click on the Status tab then choose OpenVPN. It should show 'Connected Success' on the client state.


  17. Open another browser tab and go to ipleak.net to check on your connection status if you are connected to the right virtual location. In this example, it is connected to the US virtual location and there are no webRTC and DNS leaks
