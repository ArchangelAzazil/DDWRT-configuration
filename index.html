How to install Hotspot Shield on your DD-WRT Router




  • Please log in to your account on Hotspot Shield website Hotspot Shield


  • Go to your home page, click on Hotspot Shield VPN on the left side, below your Home Icon
  • Scroll down until you see the 'Download now' button. Click on 'Router'.


  • Select your preferred virtual location and click on 'Download file'. Take note of the Username and
  • Password. Please don't close this page. Go back to the router setup page.


  • We are using the Netgear router R7000 with DD-WRT Firmware Version 3.0-r53714 (10/20/23)
  • Connect your router using a LAN cable to your ISP Modem


  • Please make sure that IPV6 is disabled and click 'Save'


  • Click on the Services tab and choose VPN


  • Enable the OpenVPN Client. You will find that a lot of the fields are populated with default values.
  • We can ignore them for now. They will be changed later. Scroll Down until you reach the
  • 'Import Configuration' option.


  • Click on 'Choose File' and select the configuration you downloaded
  • from the Hotspot Shield website and click on 'Open'


  • As soon as you click 'Open', you will find that most of the fields are now populated with the
  • required configuration for our router, including the certificates, public and private key.
  • You do not need to change any of these. But you will need to enter your Username and Password.


  • Go back to the Hotspot Sheild website where you downloaded the configuration file. Copy
  • the Username and Password and enter them in the Username and Password fields for the router.


  • Click on 'Save' then click on 'Apply Settings'. Please give this a few seconds to update.


  • Click on the Status tab then choose OpenVPN. It should show 'Connected Success' on the client state.


  • Open another browser tab and go to ipleak.net to check on your connection status
  • if you are connected to the right virtual location. In this example,
  • it is connected to the US virtual location and there are no webRTC or DNS leaks.
